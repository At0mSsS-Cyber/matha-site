Frequently asked questions

How many cameras do I need?

It depends on your entry points and blind spots. Most homes start with 2–4 cameras and shops with 4–8. We'll recommend the right number — free — during a site survey.

Can I watch the cameras live on my phone?

Yes. Every system we install is set up with a mobile app for live viewing and playback from anywhere, on both Android and iPhone.

How many days of recording do I get?

Usually 15–30 days, depending on the number of cameras, resolution and hard-disk size. We size the storage to the retention period you want.

What happens during a power cut?

We can add a UPS or inverter backup so your cameras and recorder keep running and recording through outages.

Do you handle installation and wiring?

Yes — every package includes professional wiring, mounting, configuration and a full walkthrough. No hidden charges.

Do you offer warranty and maintenance (AMC)?

All products carry manufacturer warranty, and we offer Annual Maintenance Contracts for worry-free upkeep and priority support.
